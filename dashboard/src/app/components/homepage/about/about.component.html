About Us

Our passion is to ensure your success as a trader

We strive towards knowing that everyone who is part of our company, has the right tools and support to become a disciplined and consistent trader.

Why did we create FFN?
The simple answer… We, want to help retail traders get funded and earn a consistent living through trading larger amounts of capital without risking their own money.

As traders that have gone through the testing process time and time again, we saw an opportunity to make it better… much better.

(The more in depth answer) We as the founders came together with the general idea that we want to build a company that is truly focused on helping traders get funded, instead of just charging a subscription and wishing you the best of luck. At FFN, we know that it needs to be much more than that. We sum this up in our core values.
Integrity
The greatness of a man is not in how much wealth he acquires, but in his integrity and his ability to affect those around him positively. – Bob Marley.

At FFN, being fair and honest with our traders is the only way we will operate.
Transparency
We want to create an environment that is fully transparent, allowing all of our traders to have full faith in the process. How does the testing work? How do I get paid? Are there any erroneous stipulations before I can withdraw money? We are an open book. If you have questions that are not addressed on our website, please reach out to us! We want to answer your questions.
Discipline
Knowing when to trade and when not to trade, knowing when to enter and when to exit, knowing when to take a walk, or even stop trading for the day. There are ZERO successful traders who don’t practice discipline. (Read that sentence a few more times) Not only do we want a network of humble traders, we also want a network of disciplined traders.
Accountability
Last but not least, we want to create a network that thrives on accountability, just like you would encounter at a brick and mortar prop desk. Well all need accountability as traders. Trading is you vs. yourself. Having an accountability partner/s will stop you from making bad decisions, and will also be there when you need someone to talk to, who understands what you are experiencing as a trader.

Our Story

The story is fairly simple… we are just a couple of average Joes that happened to bond over our love of trading. (Jay has been trading and mentoring traders for 10+ years. Jay mentored both Kevin and Kyle which is how they all met, and began trading together). Each morning we all hop on discord around 8:30, and we trade the same way that we would in a brick and mortar prop firm. Conversation is light, and mostly focused on the products we are trading. Over time, we’ve discussed which prop firms we like/dislike etc… Through those conversations, FFN was born. As traders that have been involved with many prop firms over the years we know what is important to us (YOU) as the trader, so we decided to create a company based on exactly that. Without the traders, capital cannot be deployed. We want you the trader to be the number one focus. We want you to have clarity on what exactly you’re doing, how it works and how you can be successful.
