How It Works

Choose your account size

Pay the monthly subscription & start your evaluation account

Passing the evaluation

Reach your Profit Target without breaking any rules

If a rule is broken, you will have the option to restart a fresh evaluation for a 100$ reset fee.

Exhibition Account

Once your evaluation is complete and audited, you will move on to the Exhibition account.

Once you have completed the evaluation and your account has been audited you will receive your Exhibition account

Wait time

Trade the "Exhibition" account while your "Funded-Pro" account is setup for live trading. (10-15 business days).

If a rule is broken during the "exhibition", you must return to the Evaluation account and restart.

Transfers

You may transfer up to the pre-determined amount relative to your account size, from "exhibition" to "Funded-Pro".

Withdrawls

Withdrawals of (minimum) 1k+ can be made every Tuesday provided you are above the account threshold.

When a withdrawal is initiated, FFN will take 20%, and distribute the remaining 80% via PayPal, bank wire, or ACH (ach by default).

There is no maximum withdrawal, as long as you remain above your account threshold.

Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours but may take up to 7 business days to reach your bank.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Am I (The Trader) responsible for fees?

    During your evaluation period, you will not pay live fees or commissions because your evaluation takes place in a simulated market environment. However, the fees and commissions WILL be calculated and applied to your evaluation SIM balance, so it’s important when tracking your PnL to account for the costs involved in each transaction.

  • Am I allowed to trade Micro products? Which Micros do I have access to?

    Yes, you may trade E-micro products such as MES, MNQ, M2K, MYM, MCL, MBT, and MET. If there is a particular Micro product that you wish to trade, and it is not listed as one of our available products, feel free to reach out to the support team and we are likely to make the necessary adjustments for you to trade that product. We currently list Micro ES, Micro NQ, Micro Dow, Micro Crude Oil, Micro Bitcoin,Micro Eth, and soon to come Nano-BTC (an even smaller Bitcoin contract!)

  • Can I trade during high impact news events?

    During the Evaluation period : Yes While trading the Exhibition Account or Pro-Funded Account : Yes – however you must be FLAT 2 minutes before and 2 minutes after ANY scheduled Federal Reserve/FOMC related news event, and you must be FLAT 2 minutes before and 2 minutes after EVERY Non-Farm-Payroll release. Please reference the economic calendar and pay close attention to these events, as an open position during said events will result in loss of permission to trade funded accounts. The economic calendar that we reference can be found: HERE.

  • Can I hold trades overnight, or through multiple trading sessions?

    No. ALL positions opened by our traders MUST be closed by 03:55pm EST. Traders are permitted to trade DURING the overnight session if they wish to do so, beginning at 06:00 pm EST, all the way through to 03:55 pm EST the following day.
